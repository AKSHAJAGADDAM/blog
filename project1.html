QR-Code Generator Project

Introduction:
Quick Response (QR) codes seem to appear everywhere these days. We can see them on posters, magazine ads, websites, product packaging and so on. Using the QR codes is one of the most intriguing ways of digitally connecting consumers to the internet via mobile phones since the mobile phones have become a basic necessity thing of everyone. We are creating QR codes by which the users enter text into a web browser and get the QR code generated. QR codes, short for Quick Response codes, are two-dimensional barcodes that contain encrypted information. Initially developed for inventory management, QR codes have now become ubiquitous in advertising, product packaging, and ticketing systems. The purpose of a QR code generator is to provide a tool for easily creating QR codes that can store and convey various types of information, facilitating efficient data transfer in a wide range of applications and industries.
